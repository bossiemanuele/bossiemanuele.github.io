I am excited to share that our paper, **“A Sensor-Scheduling Approach to Predict Human Reliance During Automated Driving,”** has been accepted to the **IFAC World Congress 2026**.

This work, completed during my time at Purdue University, explores how adaptive sensor-scheduling strategies can be used to estimate and predict human reliance in automated driving environments, contributing to safer and more effective human–machine interaction in safety-critical systems.

I am sincerely grateful to my collaborator Sibi and and mentor Dr. Jain for their guidance and support throughout this research.
